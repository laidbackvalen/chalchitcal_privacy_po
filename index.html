Privacy Policy

Effective Date:13 August 2024

Thank you for using Chalchitra Call App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by our app and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

Our app requires certain permissions to function correctly. These permissions allow us to collect and use information as described below:

Camera

The camera permission is used to capture video during video calls. The video feed is transmitted in real-time to other participants in the call and is stored locally on your device while the app is in use. We do not store or share your video recordings with third parties without your consent.

Microphone

The microphone permission allows us to record audio during video calls and voice messages. This audio is used to facilitate real-time communication between users and is stored locally on your device only while the app is in use. We do not share this audio with third parties without your consent.

Telephone

The telephone permission is used to manage call functionality, such as detecting incoming and ongoing calls to ensure the app can properly handle call interruptions and maintain the quality of video and audio during active calls. This information is used solely for app functionality and is not shared with third parties without your consent.

Storage

We request access to your storage to:

  • Save and access images or videos that you may share during a call.
  • Store and retrieve any media files that are part of your call history, such as saved recordings or snapshots, if applicable.

These actions are necessary for maintaining your call history and media content. We do not use this access for any other purpose.

Other Permissions

Our app also requires permissions to:

  • Run foreground services to ensure the app remains active and functional during video calls.
  • Control vibration to notify you of incoming calls or other important events within the app.
  • Have full network access, view network connections, and view Wi-Fi connections to maintain a stable and secure connection for video calls.
  • Change your audio settings and pair with Bluetooth devices to allow seamless integration with external audio equipment, such as headsets and speakers, for an improved calling experience.

These permissions are used to enhance the functionality and user experience of the app. We do not use this information for any purpose other than described above.

2. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our app, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
